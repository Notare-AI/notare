## Taming the Research Beast

Every research project starts with a mountain of documents. The real challenge isn't just reading them—it's understanding the connections between them. This is where Notare shines.

### Step 1: Upload Your Sources

Gather all your PDFs and upload them to a new canvas in Notare. Our PDF viewer keeps everything in one place, so you can stop juggling dozens of tabs.

### Step 2: Use AI for a First Pass

Don't have time to read every word? Use the **TLDR** and **Key Points** features to get a high-level overview of each document. This helps you quickly identify the most relevant papers.

### Step 3: Start Connecting Ideas

As you read, highlight text and create notes. Drag and drop these notes onto the canvas and draw connections between them. You'll start to see your thesis form visually.

This process transforms research from a linear, tedious task into a dynamic, creative one. Give it a try on your next project!
