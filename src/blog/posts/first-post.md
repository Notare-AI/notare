## Welcome!

This is the very first post on the Notare blog. Our mission is to build the most intuitive and powerful tool for turning your research, documents, and ideas into a connected web of knowledge.

### What is Notare?

Notare is an intelligent canvas designed for students, researchers, and lifelong learners. It allows you to:

- Upload and interact with your PDF documents directly.
- Use AI to generate summaries, extract key points, and create references.
- Build visual knowledge maps to connect ideas in a way that makes sense to you.

We're just getting started, and we're so excited to have you on this journey with us. Stay tuned for more updates, tutorials, and insights into better knowledge management!
